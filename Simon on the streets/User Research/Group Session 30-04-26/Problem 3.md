# Offline access was seen as important

We thought that due to the possibilities of data being inaccurate, that a couple of methods could be used to identify people who the charity has spoken to:

* Name and DOB would be sought, but that’s apparently not accurate all of the time
* Location of the encounter
* Photo of the individual

Location was thought to be the most likely reliable source of information when referring back to a case or trying to search for someone the charity has spoken to previously.

We discussed preloading data in the area the charity worker is operating in, so that they could locate and work with a record even if their device had no reception

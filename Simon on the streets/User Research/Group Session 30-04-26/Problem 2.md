# How might we reduce the number of steps, fields and decisions needed to record a simple case note or action?

### Simplification 
* Review & rationalise the information being asked for

### Consider the data capture mechanism
* Audio capture with added translation
* Speech to Text

### Streamline the navigation
* Widget/tile to navigate to different sections
* Search functionality
* Pre-filled templates with dropdown
* Free-text field option
* UI/UX - user interface, user experience design - simple (GDS?)

### Leverage AI
* AI agent to record speech & auto populate fields.
* Auto-complete linked to AI (i.e. anticipating responses)
* AI agent integration linked to summary & coded responses for reporting purposes.

### Physical device considerations
* Can the case worker use tablets rather than a mobile device?
* Do case workers use personal devices or are they standardised?

### Connectivity considerations
* Ability to make notes in 'off-line' mode. This could also be voice recording.
* Offline data processing.
* Ability to upload (and interpret) a variety of file formats, eg: messages, pictures, voice etc.
* Have the option to send info to a generic number or generic email address through other apps, e.g. whatsapp, email, voice notes, call. The receiving system could then process it and populate the case management system.

### Other items
* Video option for sign-language
* Data consent option
